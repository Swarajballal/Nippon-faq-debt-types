Overnight Fund

As the name suggests, these funds have a maturity of 1 day. They can carry minimal credit and interest rate risks due to the short maturity and hence could be perceived as stable.

Ultra-Short Duration Fund

These debt mutual funds invest in debt securities and money market instruments in a way that the Macaulay Duration* of the portfolio is between 3 to 6 months. These can be suitable for conservative investors.

Low Duration Fund

These funds invest in debt securities and money market instruments in a way that the Macaulay Duration of the portfolio is between 6 to 12 months. This may suit investors who want to park their surplus money for a short term.

Short Duration Fund

These funds invest in debt securities and money market instruments in a way that the Macaulay Duration of the portfolio is between 1 to 3 years. This is ideal for investors who can invest money they don't need for at least 12 to 18 months.

Medium Duration Fund

These funds invest in debt securities and money market instruments in a way that Macaulay Duration of the portfolio is between 3 to 4 years. Suitable for those want to park their surplus money for 3 to 4 years horizon.

Money Market Fund

These funds invest in money market securities with a maximum of 1-year maturity. This fund can be used as an emergency fund as it can be highly liquid with relatively good risk adjusted returns.
